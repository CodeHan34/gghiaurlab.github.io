Our Research

We study the molecular and cellular biology of hematopoietic stem cells (HSCs), the bone marrow niche, and leukemic transformation. Our work spans:

  • Microenvironmental regulation of HSCs
  • Mechanisms of resistance to leukemia therapy
  • Cellular cross-talk in bone marrow niches
